Tutankhamun
Tutankhamun, also known as Tutankhamen, lived around 1341 BC to 1323 BC.
He was an ancient Egyptian pharaoh.
Tutankhamun ruled Egypt from approximately 1332 to 1323 BC.
His reign was part of the late Eighteenth Dynasty of ancient Egypt.
He was born with the name Tutankhaten.
Tutankhamun was likely a son of the pharaoh Akhenaten, who is thought to be the KV55 mummy.
His mother was identified through DNA testing as The Younger Lady, who is buried in KV35.
She was a full sister of her husband.
